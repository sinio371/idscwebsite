MOA signing between PNP Albay PPO and Infotech Development Systems Colleges Inc. (IDSC)

MOA signing between PNP Albay PPO and Infotech Development Systems Colleges Inc. (IDSC) At about 8:00 AM of August 1, 2022, Albay PPO, duly represented by PCOL RICHARD M AQUITANIA, OIC, entered into an agreement with the IDSC, represented by its President, PBGEN JOSE A RONCESVALLES (Ret), on the granting of discounts of tuition fees for all personnel of Albay PPO and their immediate family members. The signing of MOA was witnessed by Dr. Florivel D. Villacorta, VP for Academic Affairs, IDSC, and PLTCOL RUEL M PEDRO, Chief, PCADU, Albay PPO, all personnel of Albay PPO and staff of IDSC during the Traditional Monday Flag Raising Ceremony, held at Albay PPO, Camp Captain Salvador Jaucian Del Rosarion Sr., Brgy. Sta. Cruz, Ligao City. Another manifestation of strong partnership with the academe, which will pave the way for academic enhancement of PNP personnel and their children, and the promotion of BS Criminology program in the community.
